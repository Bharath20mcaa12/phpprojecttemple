Kalasa


    Santara dynasty

  • The history of this region is also associated with Santara or Bhairarasa dynasty.
  • Santara or Bhairarasa is the name of a medieval ruling dynasty of Karnataka, India.


    Transport

  • Kalasa lies amidst Malenadu at a distance of 119 km from Mangalore, 310 km from Bengaluru and 85 km from district headquarters Chikkamagaluru. Horanadu is at a distance of 9 km.
  • Kalasa can be reached by KSRTC and private buses.
  • Nearest railway station is Chikkamagaluru railway station.
  • The nearest airport is Mangalore International Airport previously known as Bajpe airport situated in Mangalore.
